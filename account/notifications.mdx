The Notifications page is your activity feed for everything happening in your workspace. Access it from the bell icon in the bottom-left sidebar.

## Notification types

Use the filter tabs at the top of the page to focus on a specific category:

| Tab | What it shows |
|-----|--------------|
| **All** | Every recent notification in your workspace |
| **Unread** | Notifications you haven't opened yet |
| **Comments** | Mentions and comment threads on proposals you are part of |
| **Scout AI Research** | Alerts from Scout when new funding opportunities are found |

### Comments and mentions

Comment notifications are generated when:

- A teammate mentions you with `@yourname` in a proposal or comment
- A new reply is added to a comment thread you're participating in

Each notification links directly back to the relevant proposal and the specific passage where the comment was made.

### Scout AI research alerts

Scout AI sends two types of funding notifications:

- **Search complete** — triggered when a manual Scout search finishes, with a link to view the results
- **Weekly digest** — a curated list of new funding opportunities matching your organization's profile, delivered on a regular schedule

Click **View Opportunities** in a digest notification to open the search results filtered to that week's matches.

## Managing notifications

From the toolbar at the top of the Notifications page:

- **Mark all as read** — clears the unread count in one click
- **Delete all** — permanently removes all notifications from your feed

## Notification preferences

To control which alerts you receive, navigate to **Settings → Preferences**. You can toggle:

| Setting | What it controls |
|---------|-----------------|
| **Enable Notifications** | General workspace and system notifications |
| **Proposal Reminders** | Reminders about approaching proposal deadlines |
| **Funding Alerts** | Notifications when Scout AI finds new matching opportunities |

Click **Save Preferences** after making changes.
