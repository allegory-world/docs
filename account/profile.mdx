---
title: "Profile"
description: "Update your personal information and manage your account."
---

Your Profile page lets you update your personal details and control your account. Navigate to it by clicking your avatar or name in the bottom-left corner of the sidebar.

## Profile information

| Field | Editable | Notes |
|-------|----------|-------|
| **Email** | No | Contact support to change your email address |
| **Full Name** | Yes | Displayed in comments, mentions, and assignment fields |
| **Job Role** | Yes | Visible to teammates in the member list |

After making changes, click **Save Changes**. The button only appears when you have unsaved edits—click **Reset** to discard them.

## Password

To change your password, click **Reset Password**. Allegory will send a password reset link to your registered email address. Follow the link to set a new password.

## Sign out

Click **Sign Out** to end your current session. You will be redirected to the login page.
