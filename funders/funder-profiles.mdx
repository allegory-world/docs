The Funders Database is Allegory's directory of verified grant-making organisations. Navigate to **Funders** in the left-hand sidebar to access it.

<Note>
  The Funders Database is currently in beta. Coverage and features will expand over time.
</Note>

## What's in the database

The database surfaces verified funders only — organisations confirmed as active grant-makers or funding sources. Unverified organisations are excluded from search results.

Funders are categorised by type:

| Type | Description |
|------|-------------|
| **Foundation** | Public charitable foundations that make grants |
| **Private Foundation** | Family or corporate foundations with endowments |
| **Impact Investor** | Investors seeking social or environmental returns alongside financial ones |
| **Venture Capital** | VC firms with grant or non-dilutive funding programmes |
| **Government** | National, regional, or municipal government funders |
| **Corporate** | Companies with formal CSR or grant-making programmes |
| **Donor Advised Fund** | Pooled giving vehicles managed by sponsors like community foundations |

## Searching and filtering

The search bar at the top of the page runs a live search as you type — no need to press Enter. Use the filter dropdowns to narrow results further:

- **Type** — filter to a specific funder category
- **Country** — filter by United States or United Kingdom
- **Focus Area** — multi-select filter across sectors including Human Services, Education, Health, Environment, Arts & Culture, and Community Development

Use the **Sort** control to order results alphabetically (A–Z or Z–A) and the **Per page** control to show 25, 50, or 100 results at a time.

Click **Reset** to clear all active filters and return to the full results.

## Saving a funder

Click the **heart** icon on any card or inside a funder profile to save that funder to your list. Saved funders appear in your [Funding Tracker](/funding-tracker/funding-opportunities) CRM under Funders, where you can add notes and track your relationship.

To unsave a funder, click the heart icon again. You will be prompted to confirm — any associated data is archived and restored automatically if you save the funder again.

## Funder profile

Clicking a funder opens their full profile, organised into tabs.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Overview tab">
    The Overview tab shows:

    - **About** — the funder's full description and mission
    - **Funding Insights** — aggregated stats from their grant history: total funding distributed, average grant per recipient, and median grant per recipient
    - **Funding by Year** — a line chart showing how their total funding, average grant size, and median grant size have changed year on year
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Opportunities tab">
    Known grant opportunities and programmes from this funder, drawn from Allegory's database. Each opportunity shows its name, status, funding range, deadline, and a link to the source where available.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="People tab">
    Key people at the funder organisation, including their title or headline and a link to their LinkedIn profile where available. People data is enriched automatically and updated as new information becomes available.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Funding History tab">
    A full record of this funder's historical grants to other organisations, sourced from public filing data. The table shows:

    | Column | Description |
    |--------|-------------|
    | **Year** | The tax year the grant was recorded |
    | **Recipient** | Name of the recipient organisation, with their EIN below if available |
    | **Purpose** | The stated purpose or description of the grant |
    | **Amount** | Cash amount, with non-cash value shown separately where applicable |

    **Filter by year** using the year selector to focus on a specific filing period. **Search by recipient or purpose** using the search box to find specific grants within the history.

    **Clicking a recipient's EIN** opens a profile panel for that organisation, showing their type, country, focus areas, and social links if they exist in the Allegory database. This lets you quickly understand who else this funder has supported without leaving the page.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

The sidebar on the right-hand side of every funder profile shows a quick-reference panel with the funder's status, type, country, EIN (for US organisations), focus areas, and links to their website, social profiles, and Charity Navigator listing where available.
