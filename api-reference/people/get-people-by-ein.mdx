---
openapi: GET /v1/funders/{ein}/people
---

Retrieve contacts associated with a nonprofit funder by their EIN. Data may be sourced from regulatory filings, manual entry, or other integrations, and enriched with AI search.

## Data sources

Contact data comes from multiple sources:

- **990**: Officers, directors, and key employees from regulatory filings
- **manual**: Contacts added manually through the Allegory platform
- **exa**: Contacts discovered through AI-powered web search

## Examples

<RequestExample>
```bash cURL
curl -X GET 'https://api.allegory.world/v1/funders/131635294/people?limit=50' \
  -H 'X-API-Key: YOUR_API_KEY' \
  -H 'Accept: application/json'
```
</RequestExample>
