---
title: "Introduction"
description: "Allegory is an AI-powered grant management platform built for teams who want to find the right funding and win it."
---

Allegory brings together a verified database of tens of thousands of funders, an AI research agent, and a collaborative proposal workspace — so your team can spend less time searching and more time writing compelling applications.

Scout, our AI agent, finds opportunities across global grant databases and the live web, answers your questions about funders and eligibility, and drafts proposals grounded in your organisation's actual documents and history. Everything it discovers or produces flows directly into your Funding Tracker, where your team can collaborate in real time.
