---
title: "Team Funding Tracker"
description: "Give your team a focused view of only the grant opportunities relevant to them, keeping Scout AI's context targeted and your pipeline organised."
---

The Team Funding Tracker is a filtered view of your organisation's pipeline that shows only the opportunities assigned to a specific team. Team members see exactly what they need to work on without the distraction of the wider organisation's activity.

Access it from the left-hand sidebar under **Your teams** → expand a team → **Funding Tracker**.

<Note>
  The global Funding Tracker (visible to Owners and Admins) always shows all opportunities across the organisation. The Team Funding Tracker is an additional scoped view, not a replacement.
</Note>

## Assigning an opportunity to a team

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the global Funding Tracker">
    Navigate to **Funding Tracker** in the left-hand sidebar. This is the organisation-level view showing all opportunities.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open the actions menu">
    Click the **⋯** menu on the right side of the opportunity row.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add to team">
    Select **Add to Team** and choose the team from the submenu. An opportunity can be assigned to more than one team at the same time.
  </Step>
</Steps>

The opportunity will now appear in that team's Funding Tracker view.

## Removing an opportunity from a team

Navigate to the team's Funding Tracker view, open the **⋯** menu on the opportunity row, and select **Remove from Team**. This removes it from the team's view only—the opportunity remains in the organisation-level tracker.

## Organisation vs. team level

| Level | What you see |
|-------|-------------|
| **Organisation** | All active funding pipelines across the entire organisation |
| **Team** | Only the opportunities explicitly assigned to that team |
