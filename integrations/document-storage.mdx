---
title: "Document Storage"
description: "Connect Allegory to your existing file storage to import documents and give Scout AI the context it needs."
---

Connecting a document storage integration lets you import files directly from your existing tools rather than uploading them manually. Once imported, documents appear in your [Documents](/documents/managing-documents) repository where you can manage AI Context and team assignments as normal.

To connect an integration, navigate to **Settings → Integrations**.

## Notion

Allegory connects to Notion directly. You can set it up using either an **Internal Integration Token** (simpler, no developer setup required) or **OAuth** (for more advanced or shared configurations).

### Internal Integration Token (recommended)

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create a Notion integration">
    In Notion, go to [www.notion.so/profile/integrations](https://www.notion.so/profile/integrations) and click **New integration**. Give it a name, associate it with your workspace, and copy the **Internal Integration Token** that is generated.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect in Allegory">
    On the Notion card in Settings → Integrations, click **Enable**. Select **Internal Integration** as the type, paste your token, and confirm.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Share pages with your integration">
    Back in Notion, open each page or database you want to import. Click **⋯** → **Connect to** and select the integration you created. Notion only shares content you explicitly grant access to.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select pages to import">
    In Allegory, the page selector will open automatically after connecting. Choose the pages and databases you want to import into your Documents repository.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### OAuth

If your organisation uses OAuth-based Notion connections, click **Enable** on the Notion card, select **OAuth**, enter your Notion OAuth credentials (Client ID, Client Secret, and Redirect URI), and follow the authorisation flow.

---

## Google Drive, Dropbox, Box, OneDrive, and SharePoint

These integrations follow the same connection flow.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click Enable">
    On the relevant integration card in Settings → Integrations, click **Enable**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Authenticate">
    A connection window will open. Sign in with the account you want to connect and grant the requested permissions.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select files">
    A file picker will open. Select the individual files or folders you want to import into Allegory. You can select multiple files at once.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Sync completes">
    Selected files are synced to your Documents repository. This may take a moment depending on the number and size of files selected.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Adding more files from a connected integration

Once an integration is connected, click **Configure** on its card to reopen the file picker and import additional files.

### Disconnecting

Click **Disconnect** on any connected integration card to remove the connection. Previously imported files remain in your Documents repository.

<Note>
  Only one cloud storage integration (Google Drive, Dropbox, Box, OneDrive, or SharePoint) can be connected at a time. Disconnect your current integration before connecting a different one. Notion can remain connected independently.
</Note>
