The Billing tab shows your active subscription, usage against your plan's limits, and gives you access to Stripe's secure customer portal to manage payment details.

## Subscription status

The top section of the Billing tab shows:

- **Plan name** and current status badge — one of Active, Free Trial, Paused, Past Due, Cancelled, or No Subscription
- **Current billing period** — the start and end dates of your current cycle
- **Payment method** — the card brand and last four digits on file
- **Usage** — current member and team counts relative to your plan limits

If you are approaching a limit, a warning banner appears with the number of slots remaining.

## Managing your subscription

Click **Manage Billing** to open the Stripe customer portal. From there you can:

- Update your payment method or billing details
- Download past invoices
- Upgrade or downgrade your plan
- Cancel your subscription

Changes made in the portal take effect at the start of the next billing cycle unless otherwise noted.

<Note>
  If you don't have an active subscription yet, a **Subscribe** button will appear instead. Clicking it takes you through Stripe's checkout flow to start a new subscription.
</Note>

## Referral codes

### Applying a referral code

If someone shared a referral code with you, enter it in the **Referral Code** field and click **Apply**. A valid code applies a discount to your next invoice.

Common errors:

| Error | Meaning |
|-------|---------|
| Invalid referral code | The code doesn't exist or has already expired |
| Already applied | A code has already been redeemed on this subscription |
| Self-referral | You cannot use your own referral code |
| No active subscription | There is no active or trial subscription to apply the code to |

### Sharing your referral code

Once you have made at least one payment, a referral code is generated for your organization. Share it with others—when they use it, you earn credits toward your subscription. Your referral code, the number of credits remaining, and a list of successful redemptions are displayed in the **Referral** section below the subscription card.

Click the copy icon next to your code to copy it to clipboard.
