The Members & Teams section has two tabs: **Members** for managing who has access to your workspace, and **Teams** for organizing members into focused groups.

## Members

### Roles

Each member in your organization has one of three roles:

| Role | Description |
|------|-------------|
| **Owner** | Full access, including billing and the ability to assign any role |
| **Admin** | Can manage members, teams, and organization settings |
| **Member** | Standard access to features; cannot manage other members or settings |

There must always be at least one Owner in the organization. The last Owner cannot be demoted or removed.

### Inviting a member

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the invite form">
    Click **Add member** in the top-right corner of the Members tab.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter their email address">
    Type the email address of the person you want to invite.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set their role">
    Choose **Owner**, **Admin**, or **Member** from the role dropdown.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Send the invitation">
    Click **Send invitation**. The person will receive an email with a link to join your workspace.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Pending invitations appear in a separate table below the member list. You can revoke a pending invitation at any time by clicking the remove icon next to it.

### Managing existing members

The member table shows each person's **email**, **role**, **join date**, and available actions. If you have permission, you can:

- **Change a role** — click the role dropdown in the member's row and select a new one
- **Remove a member** — click the remove icon; this revokes their access immediately

<Note>
  Your plan determines the maximum number of members you can invite. A warning will appear when you are approaching your limit.
</Note>

## Teams

Teams let you organize members into focused groups—for example, a team dedicated to a specific programme or funding stream. Permissions and funding opportunities can be scoped to a team.

### Creating a team

Click **Create team**, enter a name, and click **Create team** to confirm. You are automatically added as an Admin of the new team.

### Managing team members

Click the **manage members** icon next to any team to open the team member dialog. From there you can:

- **Add members** — select any organization member not already in the team and assign them a team role (Admin or Member)
- **Change a member's team role** — use the role dropdown next to their name
- **Remove a member from the team** — click the remove icon

Team roles (Admin and Member) are independent of organization roles and apply only within that team.

### Renaming or deleting a team

- To rename: click the **edit** icon next to the team name, update it, and confirm with the checkmark
- To delete: click the **delete** icon. This removes all team memberships and cannot be undone

<Note>
  Your plan determines the maximum number of teams you can create. A warning will appear when you are approaching your limit.
</Note>
